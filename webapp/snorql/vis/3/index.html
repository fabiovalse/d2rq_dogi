How is structured the hierarchy of the DoGi classification? Which law branches are present?

The diagram shows the hierarchy, especially devised by DoGi, for classifying juridical acadamic articles. Each slice represents a distinct branch of the law or a specialization of it. For instance, AMM stands for Administrative Law and while its child AMM.06. stands for Administrative Justice. The width of each element depends on the:

n. of children within its sub-hierarchy (suitable for navigating the hierarchy)
n. of academic articles classified by entries in its sub-hierarchy (suitable for comparing different entries)
